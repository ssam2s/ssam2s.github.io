I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Kyungpook National University, advised by Prof. Dong-Gyu Lee at the KNU Computer Vision & Machine Intelligence Lab.

Prior to KNU, I received my B.S. degree from the Department of Computer Engineering at Kumoh National Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Hyeonju Yoon. My research focuses on human-centric AI and computer vision.
